Ellinjan Mermaid Bath Bombs for Girls

Transform your child's bath time into a magical underwater world with these enchanting mermaid bath bombs. Each of the 8 gems comes with a hidden surprise toy, making every soak an adventure. The toys are nestled inside, unveiling themselves as the bath bomb dissolves, sparking delight and imagination.

With their shimmering scale designs, these bath bombs add a touch of elegance and fantasy to any bathroom. The vibrant scents, including sweet orange, zesty lemon, and tropical coconut, are carefully crafted to create a sensory experience that kids will love.

Designed with gentle, natural ingredients, these bath bombs are safe for young skin and free from harsh chemicals or staining agents. Parents can enjoy the smooth, non-greasy formula while little ones enjoy the fizzy bubbles and colorful aromas.

Perfect for birthdays, Christmas, or Children's Day, these are ideal gifts for girls aged 3-9. Whether as a party favor, a stocking stuffer, or a special treat, they bring joy and creativity into every bath.
