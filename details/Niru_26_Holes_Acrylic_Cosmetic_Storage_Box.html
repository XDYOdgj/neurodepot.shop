Niru 26-Hole Acrylic Cosmetic Storage Box

Keep your vanity organized and stylish with this sleek, transparent organizer designed for beauty lovers. Whether you're a makeup artist or a daily user, the Niru 26-hole box is a practical and elegant solution to store your essentials in one place.

Effortless Organization

This box features 26 slots specifically designed to hold eye and lip liner products. With 20 regular pencil slots and 6 jumbo slots, it fits most eyeliner pencils on the market, ensuring your tools are safe and neatly arranged.

Durable and Safe

Made from reinforced acrylic, this organizer is stronger than glass and built to last. Its shatterproof construction makes it ideal for daily use and even for travel, keeping your makeup collection secure and protected.

Space-Saving Design

With its compact size, this cosmetic storage box fits perfectly on countertops or inside drawers. It helps reduce clutter and makes your beauty routine more efficient without compromising on style or function.
