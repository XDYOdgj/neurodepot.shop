20 Durable 2oz Clear Squeeze Bottles with Twist Top Cap

These 20-piece squeeze bottles are designed for people who want a reliable, leak-proof way to store and dispense various liquids. Built with non-toxic, commercial-grade plastic, they are safe for use in your kitchen, workshop, or even while on the go.

Key Features

  • Non-toxic materials for safe handling and use
